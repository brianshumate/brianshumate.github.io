Create-O-Plex

→  November 3, 2010   •  One minute read

Mockup concept for an idea that is no more.

Farewell…

The Create-O-Plex was just a name that I gave for a space and collection of great people with whom I once sort of belonged and worked.

I was probably the least creative of anyone there, but I enjoyed the company of everyone when it was A Thing™.

I left the Create-O-Plex and things changed after that; as people got moved around.

Trying to carry on with the name in a different way by myself was not a terribly successful venture either.

This image still makes me smile, though.
