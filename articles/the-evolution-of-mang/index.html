The Evolution of Mang

→  February 24, 2013   •  One minute read

This is where mang has arrived; stooped over and walking into hazards while actively communicating via technologies to people in ways they might not necessarily even do face-to-face on a daily basis any longer.

Fear not however, as the worlds largest advertising company is racing to save the day with a set of glasses which will complete the evolution of mang into a costly biological sensor network.

Now that’s progress!
