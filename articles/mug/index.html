MUG

by J. Brian Shumate

A sunshine perma-mug!

This above is an illustration that I commissioned the most fantastic illustrator Mr. Brandon Ortwein to create for me.

After seeing all the delightful mugs in his “The Daily Coffee” collection of work on the subject, I knew he was the one to undertake this project, and fortunately he agreed to do it!

Brandon is a real professional and complete joy to work with; I highly recommend him for illustration work if you like his style.

I used the completed illustration to make postcards, which I then gave to my immediate teammates at Basho as a token of my appreciation for them.

It carries the message “MUG” on the back of the postcard version.
