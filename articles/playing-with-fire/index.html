Playing with Fire

by Brian Shumate
December 14 2014

For CHARGAUX

Chill
    is how calmly grooving
    contentment on all faces
    around the square can
    best be described in words

Fire
    shaped like two young and
    beautiful black women who
    delicately and expertly
    shred notes immortal out
    of strings in their hands
Four
    wondrous human souls who
    lit this fire then tend to
    the heart-churning burning
    with casual determination
    and grace of immersion
Fire
    gently burns the center of
    space and casts auditory
    warmth to all who will
    receive its flickering flow
Vibe
    swaddles my heart like
    a mother's warm arms and
    kisses my face with glowing
    soothing sunshine sounds
Peace
    ringing strings and earthen
    rumbling drums sound out
    a rhythm of tranquility—
    joy delivered through music
