Asking the tough questions since 1995…

The above is both a genuine question-mark-less question and a sort of method of introspection and reflection on the state of databases, and in particular a personal favorite from the SQL variety.

It quite possibly also represents a rejected design attempt; the original intention was to actually run a Teespring (or similar) campaign with a design like the one shown above to raise awareness and funds for PostgreSQL, but that went nowhere fast.

The question will still remain valid however, and shall be asked with a grin whenever complaints and loathing about other database solutions arise.
