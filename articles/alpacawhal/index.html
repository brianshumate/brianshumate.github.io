Alpacawhal

by J. Brian Shumate

A mythical beast of supremely pointy compassion…

The Mighty Alpacawhal

I wanted my own hybrid mythical creature because unicorns and gummy bears can only take the imagination so far. I pondered on this for a few days and came to the conclusion that combining a couple actual creatures from land and sea would have the effect I wanted.

Alpacawhal is the result of my pondering and subsequent rendering and I couldn’t be more pleased with it. In my imagination, Alpacawhal is a kind and compassionate creature, and it can often be aloof or even goofy, but if you fuck with it, it will throw down, lower its head, and gore your ass away.
