Gefingerpoken Cursor Campus

  July 16, 2010     One minute read

Intermodal Steel Building Units (ISBU/shipping containers) can be arranged to fashion a small campus where web technologies could be taught.

Think of it as a neoBauhaus, green, sustainable, and progressive school for already-hippiefied open source coders who wish to learn cutting edge web design and development techniques with ample time also given for musical jamming, hacky, hiking, climbing, kayaking, longboarding, mountain biking, and the extra credit XML Parsing Masters Series of hands-on seminars.

Of course, the name gefingerpoken stems from Blinkenlights, the legendary hacker neologism for diagnostic lights:

ACHTUNG!


ALLES TURISTEN UND NONTEKNISCHEN LOOKENPEEPERS!
DAS KOMPUTERMASCHINE IST NICHT FÜR DER GEFINGERPOKEN
UND MITTENGRABEN! ODERWISE IST EASY TO SCHNAPPEN DER
SPRINGENWERK, BLOWENFUSEN UND POPPENCORKEN MIT
SPITZENSPARKSEN. IST NICHT FÜR GEWERKEN BEI DUMMKOPFEN.
DER RUBBERNECKEN SIGHTSEEREN KEEPEN DAS COTTONPICKEN
HÄNDER IN DAS POCKETS MUSS. ZO RELAXEN UND WATSCHEN
DER BLINKENLICHTEN.

The overall shaping and arrangement of the ISBU structures would, when viewed from above, strike a familiar image recognized by any web person.
